Interface Keychain<KeychainChange, DocumentKey>

A keychain contains a CollabswarmDocument's encryption keys.

Keys are identified by a key ID. In the legacy model, key IDs are 16-byte UUID v4 values. In the epoch-based model, key IDs are 32-byte SHA-256 hashes (epoch IDs).
